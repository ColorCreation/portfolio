LoZ Twilight Princess

Ilia

Ilia is the main character's best friend who grew up with him in Ordon Village. She is very fond of animals, especially your horse. She gets amnesia after she is captured by monsters.

Zelda

Zelda is the princess of the royal family of Hyrule. She was imprisoned in her own castle when Zant invaded Hyrule. Just like you, and ganondorf, she was blessed by the gods and used her powers to allow Midna to exist in the Sacred Realm.

Midna

Midna is an imp of the Twili. her kind were banished to the Twilight Realm after her ancestors tried to take over the Sacred Realm. She does not view Zant as the true king of Twilight. For some reason, she has knowledge of ancient dark magic and the history of Hyrule. It is later revealed in the game that she is the Twilight Princess.

Renado

Renado is a shaman that lives in Kakariko Village

Zant

Zant is a self-proclaimed King of Twilight. He invaded Hyrule and overthrew the royal family with his immense power. After defeating the royal family, he plunged Hyrule into the Twilight, turning all the inhabitants into small lights that are unaware of what is going on.

Ganondorf

Ganondorf is a bandit that terrorized the citizens of Hyrule with his magic. His arrogance caused him to be captured and sentenced to death. To the surprise of many, he was blessed by the gods. In order to prevent him from bringing harm to Hyrule, he was banished to the Twilight Realm. There he took refuge in Zant until he could finally manifest his body in the Sacred Realm again.
