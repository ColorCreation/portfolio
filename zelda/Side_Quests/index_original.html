LoZ Twilight Princess

Within Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess, there are multiple side quests such as:

  • The Hidden Skills
  • Agitha's Golden Bugs
  • Jovani's Soul

The Hidden Skills

There are seven hidden skills that can be learned in the game. The first one will be learned during the main quest, but the other skills will require that you seek out the Howling Stones. When you are in your wolf form, you must go near the Howling Stones and howl the tune coming from it. Once you have successfully played the tune, a wolf apparition will appear somewhere in Hyrule to teach you a one of the Hidden Skills.

Number Skill Description Location
1 Ending Blow After knocking your enemy to the ground, you immediately strike them in their heart.
  • No howling stone.
  • Learned at the entrance to the Forest Temple.
2 Shield Bash Hold out your shield, and ram it against your enemy to break their defenses.
  • The howling stone can be found along the path to Death Mountain.
  • The skill can be learned at Ordon Spring.
3 Back Slice Side step and roll till you are behind your enemy, and then perform a jump spin while slashing your enemy's back.
  • The howling stone can be found at the cliff in the Upper Zora's River near Iza's House.
  • The skill can be learned outside the east entrance to Castle Town.
4 Helm Splitter Use the Shield Bash technique to break your enemy's defenses, and then jump over them while swinging your sword to slice their helm.
  • The howling stone can be found along the path to the Sacred Grove in Faron Woods.
  • The skill can be learned near the southern entrance to Castle Town.
5 Mortal Draw Leave yourself defenseless and allow your enemies to approach you. When the time is right, draw your sword to slash through their armor.
  • The howling stone can be found on a cliff near the watchtower at Lake Hylia.
  • The skill can be learned at the entrance to the Arbiter's Grounds.
6 Jump Strike Focus on your enemy and charge your attack. When the light reaches the tip of your blade, jump and strike your nearby enemies and create a shockwave that sends them to the ground.
  • The howling stone can be found atop of Snowpeak Mountain.
  • The skill can be learned at the Graveyard in Kakariko Village, behind Chief Renado's house.
7 Great Spin Provided that your are at full health, you will be able to perform a spin attack that is more powerful than normal.
  • The howling stone can be found at Hidden Village behind a window.
  • The skill can be learned at the entrance to Hyrule Castle.

Agitha's Golden Bugs

Agitha is a girl who lives in Castle Town and has a fondness for bugs, especially golden ones. She will ask you gather these bugs for her and in return will give you Rupees in exchange. There are twelve types of golden bugs and one of each gender (a total of 24 bugs). Once you give her one bug, she will give a larger wallet to hold 600 Rupees. When you have finally given completed her quest, she will give you a giant wallet to hold 1000 Rupees.

Jovani's Soul

Jovani is a man who lives in Castle Town and was turned into a solid gold statue after selling his soul to be rich. He only wishes to returned to normal so that he can be with his girlfriend again, and will ask you to find all the poes that contain a fraction of his soul. There are sixty poes spread across Hyrule, and they only appear during the night. Once you collect twenty poe souls, Jovani will be able to move again, but will still be made of gold. When you have collected all the poe souls, he will return to normal.
