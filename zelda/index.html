LoZ Twilight Princess

The game Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess, is a game that takes place in the land of Hyrule. You play as the main character that you name. You start off as a normal villager of the Ordona Province, and are about to travel to Hyrule Castle to deliver a sword to the royal family. Little did you know that Hyrule is being invaded by strange monsters and your friends get caught in the process. As you were about to embark to Hyrule Castle, your friends get captured by monsters and just beyond the village lies a mysterious wall of darkness that grabs you into a realm of darkness. Within this realm you are afflicted by a great pain that causes you to pass out. You wake up to find yourself in a strange cage and encounter a mysterious imp. She offers to help you escape and save you r friends in exchange for your help in gathering objects that were locked away in temples. Now that Hyrule has been engulfed by the twilight, it is up to you to restore light to the land and stop the evil that plagues it.
