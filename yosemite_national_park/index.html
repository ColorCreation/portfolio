The Grand Valley Since 1864

An icon of America's majestic natural beauty, California's Yosemite National Park welcomes millions of visitors each year - drawn to its dramatic waterfalls, giant sequoias, abundant wildlife, and awe-inspiring cliffs, like Half Dome and El Capitan. Whether you're looking for the adventure vacation of a lifetime, want to immerse yourself in the stillness of nature - or simply hike, backpack, or ski in one of the most beautiful places on earth, you'll find your placec in Yosemite.
